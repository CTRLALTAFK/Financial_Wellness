Visualizing CFPB Financial Wellness Data

In 2016 the federal Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) conducted a survey of more than 6,000 Americans to assess their financial wellness. The survey used 10 basic question to product a Financial Wellness Score of between 1 and 100. A host of other demographic and financial questions provided a rich picture of the economic situation with a high level of detail and large sample size.
